Each note is a rectangle. Its x comes from the MIDI pitch (mapped to a key); its y is (note.time − now) × pxPerSec, read fresh from the Web Audio clock every requestAnimationFrame. The bar height is duration × pxPerSec. When a bar reaches the white hit line, that's the exact instant its note sounds — the visual is slaved to the audio clock, never the reverse.
